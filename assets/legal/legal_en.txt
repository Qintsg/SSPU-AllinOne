3. Privacy Policy

1. Processing Principles
The current version primarily processes data locally and does not provide developer-operated cloud synchronization. Unless you voluntarily submit feedback, logs, screenshots, or other materials, the developer does not actively collect, upload, sell, rent, share, or disclose your account credentials, campus data, email content, business cache, or personal information.

2. Purposes of Processing
This software processes necessary data only for local functions such as campus information aggregation, login state retention, local caching, notifications, app lock, WebView browsing, and user settings. Personal information processing follows the principles of lawfulness, legitimacy, necessity, minimization, and transparency.

3. Locally Stored Data
This software may locally store app settings, read status, message cache, followed items, auto-refresh settings, app lock configuration, campus card, academic, sports, and email summary cache, WebView runtime data, and WeChat Official Account configuration entered by the user. Student IDs, OA passwords, sports query passwords, email passwords, OA sessions, and other decryptable credentials required for login are stored in the operating system's secure storage.

4. Storage Locations
Desktop app data is stored by default under `.sspu-aio` in the user's directory. Android and iOS data is stored in the app support directory assigned by the operating system. The Web version uses browser local storage. On Windows, embedded WebView capabilities may store WebView2 runtime data, Cookies, cache, and site data locally.

5. Sensitive Information Notice
Account passwords, Cookies, Tokens, email credentials, course grades, campus card information, email content, identity-related information, and similar data may be sensitive or important information. Please use this software only on trusted devices and properly protect your device unlock password, system account, and app lock.

6. Network Access
To provide its functions, this software may access university websites, OA/CAS, academic systems, campus card systems, sports query systems, student affairs report systems, school email services through IMAP/POP/SMTP, WeChat Official Accounts Platform, WeChat article links, user-configured information sources, system browsers, or WebView components. When the user actively sends email through school email SMTP, recipients, cc, bcc, subject, and body are submitted to the school email service for processing. This software does not store sent-email bodies or recipient plaintext in normal local caches. Logs, Cookies, risk control, privacy policies, and data processing rules of external services are controlled by their respective providers.
